Installation

Watch John on Sanfu Youtube channel demonstrate
an installation of our spray tube

STEP 1: Drill hole into PVC pipe using a drill or hot rod

STEP 2: Insert clamp into hole

STEP 3: Insert valve at an angle smear some oil around for easy entry

STEP 4: Fix spray tube by covering the mouth of the valve and fastening the ring around it. This is to prevent any leakage. To remove the tube, just push the ring backward

STEP 5: Roll of the spray tube to the desired length

STEP 6: Use the end clip to seal the tube

Why choose our product?

Water-saving equipment for spraying irrigation that requires low water pressure (operating pressure: 0.5 to 0.8kg/cm²= 7.12 to 11.38psi) which really helps save electricity and water It sprays evenly, this helps improve the germination rate of seeds and the survival rate of seedlings

With our unique design of twin wings and a line embedded at the edge of the tube and the middle of the tube respectively, this will protect the tube from the rate of turning over and twisted, maintain the direction of water spray throughout the process of irrigation
